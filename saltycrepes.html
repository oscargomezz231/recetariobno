Preparation method

Ingredients

- 250 Ml Milk

- 150 gr wheat flour

- 1 egg

- 1 teaspoon

- 1 pinch salt

Preparation

In a bowl add the egg, the butter, the pinch of salt, the milk and beat it

We are adding the flour to the mixture little by little and beat it to form a slightly thick dough.

We spread a pan with butter and add a saucepan

When it is done on the one hand we impregnate again with butter and turn it over.

At this moment we add the filling we want and fold it in half.
