Preparation method

Ingredients - 1 cup of milk (240 ml)

- 1 cup of whipping cream (240 ml)

- 1 vanilla pod open in the middle along

- 3 yolks (60 g)

- 1/2 cup of sugar (100 gr)

- 1 pinch of salt

- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (5 ml)

- 1/2 cucarada of corn syrup (10 g)

PREPARATION

Boil the milk with the cream and the vanilla pod in a saucepan. Remove from heat and let it warm.

Beat the yolks with the sugar until they are pale and thick. Incorporate a little of the milk and cream mixture vigorously. Pour this preparation into the saucepan with the rest of the milk and mix.

Return the pan to the heat and cook over low heat without boiling, stirring constantly, until the preparation thickens and covers the back of a spoon. Add the rest of the ingredients, mix and remove from heat.

Allow to cool and refrigerate preferably overnight. Turbine the ice cream following the instructions of the ice cream machine and freeze.
