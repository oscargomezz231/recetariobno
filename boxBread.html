Method of Preparation

Ingredients

- 200 grams of flour + the necessary to knead

- 1 tablespoon of salt

- 1 tablespoon of sugar

- 1 egg

- 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil

- 1 tablespoon of yeast

- 75 ml of whole milk, at room temperature

DEVELOPMENT STEP BY STEP 1: Place the flour, salt and sugar in a bowl. Add oil, yeast, milk and egg

2: Mix with a spatula and when the ingredients are well integrated, knead for 6 minutes on a clean surface.

3: Punch the dough to eliminate the air that accumulates, stretch and fold the dough inwards along the length and then widthwise, place in a mold and let stand for half an hour.

4: Bake at 180 Â° C for 25 minutes or until the bread is golden.

5: Let cool completely and portion or reserve. Keep in a plastic bag so it does not dry
