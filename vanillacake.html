Preparation Method:

Ingredients:

3/4 cup unsalted butter

1 1/2 of granulated sugar

3 eggs

2 teaspoons of vanilla essence

BETÚN

2 cups of shortening

4 1/2 cups of sugar glass

1/2 teaspoon of meringue powder (optional)

1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence

1/4 cup of milk

PREPARATION:

- Acremer the butter at medium speed for 3 minutes until it is fluffy.

- Add the little sugar while stirring. In the same way add the eggs, one by one.

- MIX flour with baking powder and salt.

- Sift the dry ingredients and add 1/3 to the blender

- Combine milk with vanilla essence

- Beat until all ingredients are well incorporated

- Grease and flour a cake mold, pour the mixture and bake at 180 ° C for 30 minutes or until, when inserting a toothpick in the center, it comes out clean.

- Remove from the oven, let cool for 20 minutes, demold and cool completely.

- Place the shortening in the blender at medium speed for 6 minutes until no lumps remain.

- Pass the icing sugar through a sieve, add it to the butter little by little together with the powdered meringue, the vanilla essence and the milk.

- Mix until you have a smooth and creamy bitumen.

- Decorate the vanilla cake with the bitumen. You can cut the cake in half and place a thin layer of bitumen between each one.
