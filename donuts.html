Preparation method

                          

Ingredients

                          - 150 grams of butter

        - 1 on dry yeast (11 g)

        - 1 1/2 teaspoon grated orange

        - 1/4 cup of water

        - 4 1/4 cups wheat flour

        - 4 pieces egg

        - 1 can of condensed milk

        - 1 tablespoon of salt

                

PREPARATION

                           Hydrate the yeast with water, let it rest for 10 minutes. Form a source with the flour, place condensed milk, eggs, grated orange peel, butter, salt and yeast in the center.

                          Knead until you get a smooth, smooth and elastic paste, which comes off the table without difficulty. Let it rest covered with a plastic, in a warm place, until it doubles its volume.

                          On a smooth and floured surface, spread the dough with the help of a rolling pin until it is 2 cm thick. Form the donuts with a cutter for donuts, let them rest until they double their volume, fry them in hot oil, drain them.

                 Pass them through 1 cup of sugar previously mixed with 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon, shake and serve.

                          To improve the flavor it is recommended to add a bit of ground cinnamon with nuts to the dough.
