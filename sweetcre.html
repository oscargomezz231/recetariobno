Preparation method

Ingredients - 250 milliliters of milk

- 2 eggs

- 100 grams of wheat flour

- 25 grams of sugar

- 2 cuharaditas

- Butter

PREPARATION

Blend milk with eggs, flour, sugar and vanilla until you have a homogeneous mixture.

Melt a little butter in a skillet over medium-high heat and pour approximately 4 tablespoons of the dough. Move the pan to cover the entire bottom with the dough, forming a very thin layer, and cook for 2 to 4 minutes, turning once, until they look slightly golden brown.

Repeat the procedure until the mass is finished. Serve immediately or freeze, separating the crepas with each other with paper
