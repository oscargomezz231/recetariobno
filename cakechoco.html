Preparation method

Ingredients

- 1 cup of flour

- 1 cup of sugar

- 1/4 cup cocoa powder

- 1 teaspoon baking powder

- 1 teaspoon of baking soda

- 3/4 cup of milk

- 1/4 cup of soft butter

- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

- 1 egg

- 1/4 teaspoon of salt

- 1 1/2 cups of icing sugar for glaze

- 125 grams of chocolate for glaze

- 3 tablespoons butter for glaze

- 3 tablespoons of water for the glaze

PREPARATION

In a bowl combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir at low speed with the blender.

Add milk, butter and vanilla. Beat with the blender at low speed, then beat 2 minutes at medium speed, add egg and beat 2 more minutes. To put in a round mold of 22 cms of diametr, greased and floured.

Bake at 350 Â° F for 30 or 35 minutes. Remove from the oven and cool on a rack for 10 minutes. Remove from the mold and allow to cool completely.

GLASS:

Melt the chocolate and butter over low heat, remove from the fugo and add sugar and hot water moving it. If necessary add more water to have the consistency of glaze

Finally glaze the cake
