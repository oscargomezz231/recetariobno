Preparation method

Ingredients - 3/4 cup of sugar

- 1 cup of milk

- 1/4 teaspoon of salt

- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder

- 3 egg yolks, lightly beaten

- 60 grams of chocolate semiamargo, chopped

- 2 cups of cream to beat

-1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

PREPARATION Mix the sugar, milk, salt and cocoa in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly.

Let it boil.

Place the yolks in a small bowl and add little by little about 1/2 cup of the previous mixture.

Pass this mixture to the pan. Heat until it thickens, but do not let it boil. Remove from the heat and add the chocolate, stirring until it has melted.

Pour into a cold bowl and refrigerate for about two hours or until it has cooled, stirring occasionally.
