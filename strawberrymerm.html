Preparation method :

Ingredients:

    10 cups whole strawberries or 6 cups crushed strawberries

    4 cups of sugar

    A package of pectin

1:Wash the strawberries

2:Remove the top of the strawberries and crush them

3:Mix a quarter cup of sugar with a pack and a half of dry pectin

4:Light the stove over medium heat

5: Boil the mixture for one minute

6: Test if the jam is thick or not (if it is not thick yet, add another quarter of pectin)

STERILIZE THE CONTAINER WHERE IT WILL BE SAVED

1: Use hot water and soap to wash the container well

2: Place a pot of water on the stove over high heat and cover them when the water is hot

3: Remove the covers when you are ready to use them

KEEP THE MARMALADE

1: Pour the jam with a spoon into the container

2:Boil water in a large pot to place the container (s) inside, there must be about 5 centimeters of water

3: Place the container (s) inside the pot, let it boil for about 10 minutes; Use the following processing times depending on the altitude:

From 0 to 300 meters boils the container for 5 minutes

From 301 to 1800 meters the container boils for 10 minutes

From 1800 meters and up boils the container for 15 minutes

4: Remove the container from boiling water

5: Make sure you seal the caps correctly

6: Ready
