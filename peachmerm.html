Preparation Method:

Ingredients:

1 1/2 kilos of peaches

1 kilo of sugar

1 cup of water

1 slice of cinnamon

DEVELOPMENT STEP BY STEP

1: Peel the peaches, remove the stones, cut them in quarters and place them in a layered container, alternating with the sugar.

2: Then, let them marinate for 1 hour and cook on high heat for 10 minutes. Add water and cinnamon.

3: Then continue cooking over low heat, stirring with a wooden spoon when it starts to thicken.

4: When this thick, leave on fire for 10 minutes more and remove from heat. Let cool and serve.
