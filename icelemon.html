Preparation method:

Ingredients

- 2 cups of water

- 1/2 cup of lemon juice

- 1 cup of sugar

- 6 pieces of cone

- to the taste of edible pearls

PREPARATION:

In a pot over medium heat heat the water with lemon juice and sugar

Chill the preparation in a bowl with ice, water and grain salt

Once cool, place in the bowl of the blender, previously frozen. Beat a few minutes and freeze for 15 minutes. Beat again and repeat until you get a snow.

Serve ice cream cones and decorate with sugar pearls.
